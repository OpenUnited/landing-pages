Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

This document should be read in conjunction with our Terms of Service. At OpenUnited, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information to enhance the quality of our services for users. We gather information in the following ways:

Information You Give Us

When you sign up for an account or participate in activities on OpenUnited, we ask for personal information such as your name, email address, and location. If you engage in our platform's sharing features, we may ask you to create a profile that includes your name, photo, and professional details.

Information We Obtain from Your Use of Our Services

We collect data on the services you use and how you use them. This may include details like IP address, browser type, pages visited, and device information. This data helps us improve our services and provide you with a better user experience.

How We Use the Information We Collect

We use the collected information to maintain, protect, and improve our services, develop new features, and provide you with a personalized experience on OpenUnited. Specifically, we use the information for:

  • Service Delivery: Managing your account, facilitating participation in challenges, and processing rewards.
  • Support and Communication: Keeping records of your interactions with OpenUnited and using your email address to inform you about service updates, new features, or necessary changes.
  • Personalization: Offering customized experiences based on your preferences
