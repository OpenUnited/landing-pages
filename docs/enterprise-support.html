Enterprise Support

OpenUnited offers a powerful platform for optimizing digital product development by intelligently matching demand with supply through AI-powered processes. Our target enterprises typically have 250 or more employees, with at least 50 engineers involved in product development. While C-level executives, especially CFOs, are likely to see the platform as a solution to inefficiency, middle management may perceive it as a threat to their control or team structures.

To address these dynamics, our adoption strategy is flexible, allowing enterprises to engage with OpenUnited in ways that best suit their current needs and challenges. This includes introducing the platform through events like buildathons or fixathons, incentivized competitions, or cross-team collaboration overlays. Each entry point provides a way to minimize the perceived threat while demonstrating value, with the potential to scale up into a more integrated operating model over time.

Adoption Options

  • Buildathons / Fixathons: Experience the power of OpenUnited through focused events that swiftly address technical challenges. These events provide immediate, tangible results, enhancing your product development process without disrupting ongoing operations.
  • Internal Competitions: Ignite innovation within your teams by hosting competitions that inspire creativity and drive impactful solutions. OpenUnited facilitates a dynamic environment where fresh ideas flourish, boosting team morale and productivity.
  • Cross-Team Collaboration Incentives: Foster a culture of collaboration by enabling engineers to contribute across teams. With OpenUnited, your organization can seamlessly harness diverse talents, enhancing synergy and driving collective success.
  • Nearshoring Overlay: Optimize your resource allocation with OpenUnited's strategic support for nearshoring. Our platform enhances your ability to scale efficiently, ensuring cost-effective talent utilization across locations.

Why Choose OpenUnited?

Empower your leadership with a platform that aligns with your strategic goals. OpenUnited is designed to enhance efficiency and innovation, providing a seamless transition that supports your existing team structures while unlocking new opportunities for growth.

Our flexible approach allows you to engage with OpenUnited in a way that best suits your needs, whether through targeted events, competitions, or comprehensive integration. We work with you to ensure our platform complements your unique challenges and culture.

Discover how OpenUnited can be a catalyst for positive change, adding value through collaboration and innovation. Join us in transforming your enterprise into a hub of efficiency and creativity.
